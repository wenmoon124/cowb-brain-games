Breath Synchronizer
A guided breathing exercise to help you relax and focus your mind.

Inhale (4s) - The ring expands as you breathe in.
Hold (4s) - Hold your breath with the ring.
Exhale (4s) - The ring contracts as you breathe out.

Touch and hold the center ring to synchronize your breathing with the guide. Each full cycle takes 12 seconds.
